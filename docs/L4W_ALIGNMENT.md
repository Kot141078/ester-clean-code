# L4W Alignment (Witness-First Norms)

This repository uses "L4W" to mean: **L4 Reality Boundary + witness trail**.

## Norms (What We Enforce)
- **Identity:** who acts is explicit.
- **Privileges:** what may be done is explicit (least privilege).
- **Evidence:** actions produce records that survive time (tamper-evident, hash-friendly).
- **Budgets:** rate, time, and spend budgets are first-class.
- **Veto and challenge window:** humans can veto and disputed actions can be reviewed.
- **Fail-closed:** uncertain state degrades to safe behavior.

## What We Do Not Claim
- We do not claim perfect safety.
- We do not claim containment.
- We do not claim autonomy.
